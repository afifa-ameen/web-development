What is the Cancer Shield Plan about?

Aditya Birla Sun Life Insurance Cancer Shield Plan is a health insurance plan that provides with requisite fund in the hour of need and protects your personal savings in a predicament like Cancer.

Download the Product Brochure from here for more details

Key features:

  • Covers all stages of Cancer i.e. Early Stage of Cancer and Major Stage of Cancer
  • Option to choose level or increasing cover
  • Option to receive monthly income for 5 policy years on diagnosis of Major Stage of Cancer
  • Waiver of premium for 5 years on diagnosis of Early Stage of Cancer
  • Tax Benefits as per Section 80D of the Income Tax Act, 1961 as per current tax laws

What benefits does the Cancer Shield Plan offer?

Policy Benefits at different stages of Cancer:

On the diagnosis of Early Stage of Cancer, 30% of the Sum Assured on illness will be paid to you. At the Major Stage of Cancer, 100% of the Sum Assured on illness will be paid to you. This is excluding any claim paid during the Early Stage of cancer.

  • Sum Assured on Illness is the highest of:
    • 10 times the Annualized premium; or
    • 105% of all the Total Premiums Paid till the date of a valid claim; or
    • Effective Sum Assured
  • The only illness covered under this plan is Cancer.
  • Annualized Premium is the premium amount payable in a year chosen by the policyholder, excluding the taxes, rider premiums, underwriting extra premiums, GST and loadings for modal premiums, if any.
  • Total Premiums Paid means total of all the premiums received, excluding any extra premium, any rider premium and taxes.

Premium Waiver Benefit:

Once a claim under the Early Stage of Cancer Benefit is approved, all future premium payments for the policy will be waived for lower of 5 years or remaining policy term. The coverage for Major Stage of Cancer under the policy will continue until the policy terminates.

Income Benefit Option:

This benefit can be chosen at the time of policy inception and cannot be opted out from later. Under this, a monthly income equivalent to 1% of the Sum Assured on illness would be paid out once a claim for Major Stage Cancer has been admitted. This monthly income benefit will be payable for a fixed period of next 5 years even beyond the policy expiry date

When does the Cancer Shield Plan terminate?

The policy will terminate at the earliest of the following:

  • The date when claim for Major Stage of Cancer is admitted if Income Benefit Option has not been opted
  • The policy expiry date
  • The date when the last instalment is paid if Income Benefit Option is opted
  • The date on which the reinstatement period ends after your policy has been lapsed
  • On cancellation of the policy by the policyholder
  • On the date of intimation of death of the Life Insured

For more details on exclusions, please refer exclusion section in policy brochure.
